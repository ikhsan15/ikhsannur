About

I am a fresh graduate with experience in IT support and web development. In IT support roles, I handle hardware, software, and network issues to ensure smooth system operations. As a web developer, I engage in creating dynamic websites using various programming languages and frameworks such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, CodeIgniter and Yii. Contact Me!

I graduated from MAN 2 MODEL Pekanbaru in 2016 and continued my education at the Information and Communication Technology Boarding School (PeTIK), an institution in Depok, West Java, offering a program equivalent to an Associate's Degree. I chose programming because of my interest in technology and software development. This one-year program provided me with basic programming skills, including knowledge of programming languages and application development. After completing my studies at PeTIK, I had the opportunity to assist the management team with various tasks, including teaching, designing student activity programs, and creating activity documentation. In this role, I learned how to work in a team, organize events, and communicate with various stakeholders. I also became more familiar with a dynamic work environment and the importance of teamwork.

Six months later, in February 2018, I moved to Gema Insani, a publishing and printing company based in Depok. I joined the Design division, where I was responsible for creating layouts for both internal and external Qur'an products. This gave me the opportunity to explore the art of design and to develop skills in using design software like Adobe InDesign and Illustrator. I also collaborated with other teams to ensure that the designs met the company's standards. In the same year, I continued my studies at STT Terpadu Nurul Fikri in South Jakarta, pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology. This education deepened my understanding of information technology and provided me with a solid foundation in programming and system development. The following year, I joined the IT division at Gema Insani, where I was responsible for developing web applications for the company's internal needs. Additionally, I was also tasked with creating websites for the company's partners, troubleshooting PCs and networks, and managing YouTube live streaming and voice recordings. This experience strengthened my technical skills and gave me insights into various aspects of information technology and digital media.

With this background, I believe I have a combination of technical skills, creativity, and the ability to adapt to rapidly changing environments. I am always eager to learn new things and seek new challenges in my career, and I look forward to continuing my growth and making positive contributions in the future.
